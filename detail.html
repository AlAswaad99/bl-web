ZAREM ARBEGNA

International award-winning animated short

×

Around 2 million young Ethiopian women and men join the labor market every year. But there’s demand for only a third of this. Millions of unemployed youth who often lack information, skills, finance, linkage and the right mindset to become employed – this is why the BRIDGES Programme was created. Welcome to BRIDGES! The goal is to engage 530,000 youth in work, 70% women, in different sectors.
